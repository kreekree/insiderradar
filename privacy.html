Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

InsiderRadar ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website insiderradar.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Site, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using InsiderRadar, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site, our analytics providers automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Country/region (derived from anonymized IP)

Information You Provide

InsiderRadar does not require account registration or login. If you contact us via our contact page, we collect only the information you voluntarily provide (e.g., your email address and message content).

3. Analytics and Cookies

Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand site traffic and usage patterns. GA4 uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect anonymized usage data. Google may use this data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Umami Analytics

We also use Umami Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. Umami collects only aggregated, anonymized statistics about site usage.

Ezoic Advertising

We use Ezoic to display advertisements on the Site. Ezoic and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the NAI Opt-Out Page or the DAA Opt-Out Page. For more information about Ezoic's data practices, see Ezoic's Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Analyze traffic patterns to improve the Site's content and performance
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Understand which features are most used
  • Respond to contact form submissions

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for any purpose beyond operating and improving the Site.

5. External Data Sources

InsiderRadar fetches publicly available data from the US Securities and Exchange Commission's EDGAR system. When you use the Site's search tools, your browser makes API calls to InsiderRadar's servers, which in turn fetch data from SEC EDGAR. Your ticker searches or fund name queries are processed on our servers and are not sent to the SEC with any identifying information about you.

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain a user database or long-term data store. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics and Umami per their respective policies. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) to access, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Since InsiderRadar collects minimal personal data and does not maintain user accounts, the most practical way to exercise these rights is to opt out of analytics tracking (see Section 3) and to avoid submitting personal information through our contact form. For other requests, contact us at the address below.

8. Children's Privacy

InsiderRadar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this Site, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please use our contact page.
